ABOUT US


Chaitanya Kosha is an online platform where we aim at introducing Sanskrit into the lives of people through creative representation of Sanskrit wishes and greetings. It promotes the user especially the youth who have an easy access to the internet, share Sanskrit written cards to greet people, send festival wishes and spread positivity with our Sanskrit shlokas. With easy download options and user friendly interface, sharing is as easy as typing.

Pertaining to the name Chaitanya Kosha, our team took special precautions not to over flood the sections with choices. A collection of cards with our wisely chosen designs perfectly fits the requirements.

Sanskrit is one of the oldest language known to us. It is also believed to be the most systematic and technical language of all. It is also referred to as the mother of all languages and is the only language that is used in holy functions and ceremonies of the Hindus, as it has always been regarded as the sacred language of the religion. Sanskrit mantras, when recited in combination with the sound vibrations, have a specific effect on the mind and the psyche of the individual.
